Background

Who I Am

I'm Sammi, a Haitian-Nigerian creator who grew up in Brooklyn and planted roots in Atlanta five years ago. My content started in tech through tutorials, industry commentary, and building in public as a web designer and developer. That same curiosity and authenticity still drive everything I create. Outside of tech, I'm deeply connected to Caribbean culture. From carnival to soca fetes, it's the music, energy, and community I've grown up with and continue to be part of today.

Recently, I picked up a camera and started exploring event photography as a new creative outlet. While I'm a beginner behind the lens, my background in design gives me a strong visual eye for composition, storytelling, and capturing the moments people actually want to remember. I'm drawn to events that feel alive, where culture, community, and creativity naturally come together.

My audience spans two communities: Black creatives and professionals in tech who found me early, and entertainment and culture lovers including DJs, performers, event organizers, and fellow creators who connected through my lifestyle content and photography. Whether I'm creating content, designing, or behind the camera, my goal is always the same: tell stories that feel real, vibrant, and worth remembering.
